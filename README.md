About

Fusion 360 & SolidWorks CNC Machining & CAM 3D Printing MIG Welding & Sheet Metal Arduino / Embedded Systems Python & VBA Automation Rapid Prototyping Systems Modeling Mechanism Design Design for Manufacturability
I'm a mechanical engineer with a background that spans the full product development stack — from napkin sketch to CNC-machined hardware. My strongest zone is the fuzzy front end: taking problems that aren't fully defined and building the first thing that proves whether an idea is real.
Before engineering I spent six years as a fabricator building large-scale custom structures. That foundation shapes how I approach design — I think about what a part actually has to survive before I model it. At NASA Glenn I applied that same instinct to spacecraft power systems, publishing research on laser power beaming for lunar infrastructure. At Discovery Lab I do it every day, rescuing and building electromechanical exhibits that have to hold up to thousands of visitors.
I hold a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Oklahoma State and a B.S. in Health & Exercise Science from the University of Oklahoma, plus my Fundamentals of Engineering (EIT) certification.
